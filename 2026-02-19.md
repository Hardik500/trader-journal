## 4. What Didn't Work (Mistakes / Missed Opportunities)
- **Execution Hesitation:** I missed a clear ORB Breakout to the *downside*. At 9:30 AM, when Nifty broke its first 15-min candle low (near 25,780), that was a textbook "ORB Sell" signal. I hesitated because my bias was bullish and I was "hoping" for a recovery from 25,725.
- **Missed Setup:** The 11:30 AM trend continuation on the downside (BankNifty breaking 61,000) was a missed trade. By the time I regained my composure, the premiums had already doubled.
- **Bias Blindness:** I was too stuck on the "gap-up = bullish" theory and took too long to flip my bias to bearish.

---

## 5. Key Lesson
**"Trade what you see, not what you think."**
The market gave a clear signal that the gap-up was a trap (heavy selling in first 5 mins), but I let my pre-market "thought" (Bullish) blind me to what I was actually "seeing" (Bearish selloff). Next time, if the ORB breaks the opposite way of my bias, I will trust the price action over my opinion.

**Tomorrow's focus:** Be faster at identifying the "Stop Loss" of the market (the level where the bullish/bearish case is invalidated).
