## 5. Key Lesson 🔑

**During market crashes, not all sectors fall — be sector-specific, not market-direction only.**

Today proved that while Nifty crashed -3.26%, Bank Nifty closed UP +0.92%. The DII buying of ₹12,034 crore was concentrated in banks, creating a sector-specific opportunity.

**The mistake:** Being "bearish on everything" when the data showed DII-supported sectors could outperform.

**The takeaway:**
- Always check DII/FII flow by sector, not just overall market
- In a market crash, look for sectors with strong DII support (banks today)
- "No trade" is correct for direction, but could have been selective with sector exposure
- Next crash: Prepare sector-specific watchlist alongside overall market bias

> "The market can stay irrational longer than you can stay solvent — but smart money leaves breadcrumbs."
